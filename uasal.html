Space telescopes large and small. CubeSats and instrumentation. Exoplanets, debris disks, and better understanding our own planet.
Credits - Dr. Ewan Douglas

CubeSat R&D Tester

Adviser: Dr. Ewan Douglas

Development of new instruments and missions for space science to radiative transfer modeling of extrasolar star systems. Moreover, I work on the R&D of CubeSats and their prototypes and employ new methods and ideas in their creation and design. Currently, we are working on creating a 1U CubeSat payload prototype that will be flown on high-altitude weather balloons to education the K-12 and high school community about space-science research.
